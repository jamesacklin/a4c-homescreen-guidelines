Overview

These are design specifications for several independently-swappable components for the Apps4Clubz iOS client application homescreen.

The goals of these designs are to (1) provide club administrators with the means to visually distinguish their club's app from others, and to (2) provide club members with a meaningful and useful starting experience from the home screen of their club's application.

These element specifications provide a “mix and match” approach between various configurations of module navigation, welcome message, background image, and content presentation configurations,

Backgrounds

Six different background image treatments. Admins should be able to upload their own background image for the homescreen. The shortest side of the user-uploaded image should be scaled to fit within each masked area, and then cropped from the center. The background photo should also be set to 80% opacity, with a pure black (rgb(0,0,0)) background to enhance contrast with overlaid text.

There are plenty of suitable, free-to-use background images available on Unsplash.
